About Fleet Marina

Requirements to dock:

PLEASE READ BEFORE INQUIRING!

  • Proof of Insurance with a P&I Limit at a minimum of $300,000 & Pollution Limit at a minimum of $997,100. Insurance certificate MUST name Fleet Marina as additionally insured and insurance policy MUST match the same owner who is named on the contract.
  • One month's non-refundable rent payment must be submitted with contract to secure your slip.
  • Contract must be filled out entirely before docking.
  • No Vacation Rentals or Air BnB / VRBO of boats of any kind permitted within the marina.
  • No boat berthed at the marina shall be used as a residence, unless specifically authorized in writing by marina (inquire within).

Facilities:

  • Dock Carts
  • Vending Machine
  • Bags of Ice
  • Bathrooms
  • Showers
  • Free Laundry Facility
  • Dining Room
  • Off-Street Parking
  • Marina Fleet Merchandise
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi

Fleet Marina, formerly Capt. Leroy's Marina, is undergoing many renovations. Additions and upgrades are being made to the marina including the installation of the longest floating docks in New Bedford.


The owner of the marina, Lars Vinjerud II (CEO Fleet Fisheries), knows a thing or two about marinas, as he has been a professional fisherman his whole life and visited dozens of marinas all over the world in his own sport-fishing boats and yachts.


Stay tuned for upcoming changes as we make Marina Fleet the best marina in Karachi, Pakistan.
